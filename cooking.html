About This Dish

White Radish Kelp Pork Rib Soup is a classic homemade Chinese dish made with white radish, kelp, and pork ribs.

Ingredients

White Radish
A key ingredient that gives the soup a fresh taste.
Kelp
Rich in iodine and adds umami flavor.
Pork Ribs
Provides protein and makes the broth rich and flavorful.

Health Benefits

This soup is known for its cooling and lung-moisturizing effects. It provides essential nutrients such as iodine, calcium, and protein, helping to strengthen the immune system and support metabolism.
